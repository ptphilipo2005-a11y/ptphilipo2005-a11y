OUR SCHOOL

KAPT America International School is dedicated to providing a nurturing and stimulating environment for children to learn and grow. Our experienced educators are committed to fostering creativity, critical thinking, and a love for learning in every student.

Mission

  • To provide a safe and nurturing environment where children can learn and grow.
  • To treat every child with respect and kindness.
  • To foster a love for learning and creativity in every student.

    • Vison

  • To be a leading international school recognized for excellence in education.
  • To create a community of learners who are prepared to succeed in a global society.
  • To inspire lifelong learning and personal growth in every student.

    • Our Team

    Our team of dedicated educators and staff are passionate about providing the best possible education for our students. We work together to create a supportive and engaging learning environment.

    Our Facilities

    We offer state-of-the-art facilities that are designed to enhance the learning experience. Our classrooms are equipped with the latest technology, and we have ample outdoor space for play and exploration.
